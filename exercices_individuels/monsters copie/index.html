Whack That Troll!

As you wander peacefully through the forest, the rustle of leaves is suddenly broken by a deep growl. A troll steps out from behind the trees, blocking your path.

You’ll have to fight it — taking turns to attack until the creature runs out of health points. You can strike it up to six times, but be carefull, the troll will fight back !
